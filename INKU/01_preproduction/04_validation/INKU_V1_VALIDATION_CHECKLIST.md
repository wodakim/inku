# INKU V1 — Validation Checklist

## Economy
- Can player buy a slime?
- Does it fill a slot?
- Does passive income increase money?
- Does offline income apply correctly?

## Ownership
- Does slot order persist?
- Does rename persist?
- Does sell free the slot correctly?
- Does delete free the slot correctly?

## Prairie
- Can slimes be sent to prairie?
- Do they appear alive?
- Is max active count enforced?
- Does prairie remain readable on phone?

## Mental model
- Are two slimes behaviorally distinguishable?
- Do visible synthetic states match observed behavior?

## Persistence
- Does closing and reopening preserve identity?
- Is incubator offer restored coherently?
- Is save version present?
