# INKU V1 — Inter-Slime Relations

## Scope
V1 relations are contextual only.
There is no deep persistent relationship history yet.

## Goal
Make several active slimes feel aware of one another.

## Allowed reactions
- ignore
- tolerate / coexist
- soft approach
- soft avoidance

## Main drivers
- sociability
- fear
- aggression (light influence only)
- emotional stability
- stress
- social need

## Product rule
No visible relationship matrix or deep relationship UI in V1.

## Future-ready note
This contextual layer is intended to prepare later systems such as:
- familiarity
- affinity
- fear memory
- attachment
- rivalry
But none of those are part of V1.
