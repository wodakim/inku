# INKU V1 — Prairie

## Purpose
Prairie proves that owned slimes are not merely passive-income assets.

## Active limit
- maximum 3 active slimes in prairie

## Presence rule
A slime remains assigned to its slot even while active in prairie.

## Behavior library
- idle/rest
- light wandering
- local exploration
- soft approach
- soft avoidance
- player reaction

## Touch interaction
- touch
- hold
- drag
- release

## Depth rules
Depth must:
- enrich spatial readability
- support mobile clarity
- preserve slime rebound identity

## Anti-chaos rules
- no high-speed erratic movement
- no constant switching every second
- no prolonged overlap
- no aggressive chase logic in V1

## Validation target
The player should be able to say:
- this slime feels alive
- this one is calmer
- this one avoids others more
