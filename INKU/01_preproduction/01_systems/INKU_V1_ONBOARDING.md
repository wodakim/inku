# INKU V1 — Onboarding

## Principle
Teach by action, not by tutorial walls.

## Player must understand within first minutes
- slimes appear in incubator
- they cost money
- they can be bought
- they go into slots
- they generate income
- they can be inspected
- they can be sent to prairie

## Help style
Maximum 3 very short contextual hints.

## Recommended first-session sequence
1. Player enters incubator
2. Sees slime, price, money, slots, buy button
3. Buys first slime
4. Slot fills
5. Opens profile sheet
6. Optionally renames
7. Sends slime to prairie
8. Sees it move and exist

## First offer rule
Prefer first offer to be:
- Common or Uncommon
- reachable or almost reachable with starting money
