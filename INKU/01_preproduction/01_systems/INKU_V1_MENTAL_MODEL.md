# INKU V1 — Mental Model

## 3-layer structure
1. Stable traits
2. Dynamic states
3. Synthetic visible states

## Stable traits
- Curiosity
- Sociability
- Fear
- Aggression
- Attachment
- Impulsiveness
- Emotional stability

## Dynamic states
- Mood
- Energy
- Stress
- Social need
- Player trust

## Synthetic states
- Calm
- Curious
- Playful
- Tired
- Tense
- Nervous
- Unstable
- Sociable

## Behavioral mapping intent
- curiosity increases exploration tendency
- sociability increases proximity tolerance
- fear increases avoidance tendency
- aggression increases firmness but not combat
- impulsiveness increases transition irregularity
- emotional stability smooths shifts
- player trust improves handling tolerance

## V1 evolution rule
Dynamic states may change.
Stable traits remain effectively fixed in V1.
