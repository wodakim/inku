# INKU V1 — Economy

## Purpose
Economy must create desire, scarcity, and decision pressure without turning the game into a generic idle spreadsheet.

## Rarity tiers
- Common
- Uncommon
- Rare
- Epic
- Mythic

## Distribution
- Common: 52%
- Uncommon: 27%
- Rare: 14%
- Epic: 5%
- Mythic: 2%

## Starting money
- 70

## Price bands
- Common: 20–45
- Uncommon: 55–110
- Rare: 140–260
- Epic: 320–520
- Mythic: 700–1100

## Passive income / min
- Common: 0.20–0.45
- Uncommon: 0.55–1.00
- Rare: 1.20–2.20
- Epic: 2.60–4.20
- Mythic: 5.00–8.00

## Internal variation
Allowed only as light modulation:
- specimen variation within tier: moderate
- income modifier: low
- mood impact on income: very light (-5% to +5% max)

## Sell rule
- fixed 60% of purchase price in V1

## Delete rule
- no currency returned

## Incubator offer cadence
- one current offer at a time
- rotates every 3 minutes if unpurchased
- refreshes immediately after purchase

## Economic design rules
- player should not buy everything
- passive income must feel good but remain slow
- high rarity must be desirable, not progression-breaking
- 4 slots must remain meaningful as economic pressure
